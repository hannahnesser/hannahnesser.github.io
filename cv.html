Research

Harvard University   New Haven, CT

Advisor: Daniel Jacob   2017 - present

  • Works to improve constraints on the magnitude and distribution of methane emission sources using high-resolution analytic inversions of observations from the TROPOMI instrument aboard the Sentinel-5 Precursor satellite.
  • Develops and applies methods to conduct high-resolution analytic inversions atreduced computational cost while preserving information content.
  • Implements those methods to improve estimates of methane emission sources at high resolution over North America using TROPOMI observations.

Yale University   Cambridge, MA

Advisor: Drew Gentner   2015 - 2016

  • Served as Design Lead for a senior research team developing a first-generation operational prototype of portable and stationary air quality monitors measuring concentrations of greenhouse gases and EPA criteria pollutants using low-cost sensors. The sensors were designed for deployment in Baltimore, MD as part of an EPA SEARCH Center.
