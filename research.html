Research

TROPOMI Methane Concentrations
Summer 2019

My research works to improve constraints on the magnitude and distribution of methane emission sources using high-resolution, analytic inversions of observations from the TROPOMI instrument aboard the Sentinel-5 Precursor satellite. I develop and apply methods to conduct these inversions at decreased computational cost while preserving information content.
