Hannah O. Nesser

Atmospheric Inversions of Satellite Observations
PhD Candidate, Harvard University

I (she/her) am a fifth-year Ph.D. candidate in atmospheric chemistry working with Daniel Jacob at Harvard University. I work to improve constraints on the magnitude and distribution of methane emission sources using high-resolution, analytic inversions of observations from the TROPOMI instrument aboard the Sentinel-5 Precursor satellite. I develop and apply methods to conduct these inversions at reduced computational cost while preserving information content.
