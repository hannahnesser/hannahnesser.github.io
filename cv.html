Research

NASA Jet Propulsion Laboratory (JPL)   2023 - presentPasadena, CA

Advisor: Kevin Bowman

  • Works to improve constraints on the magnitude and distribution of carbon dioxide fluxes using analytic inversions of observations from the OCO-2 and OCO-3 satellite instruments.
  • Develops and applies methods to improve estimates of uncertainties in initial flux estimates and in model transport.

Harvard University   2017 - 2023New Haven, CT

Advisor: Daniel Jacob

  • Worked to improve constraints on the magnitude and distribution of methane emission sources using high-resolution analytic inversions of observations from the TROPOMI instrument aboard the Sentinel-5 Precursor satellite.
  • Developed and applied methods to conduct high-resolution analytic inversions atreduced computational cost while preserving information content.
  • Implemented those methods to improve estimates of methane emission sources at high resolution over North America using TROPOMI observations.
  • Studied the influence of boundary condition errors on regional inverse analyses.

Yale University   2015 - 2016Cambridge, MA

Advisor: Drew Gentner

  • Served as Design Lead for a senior research team developing a first-generation operational prototype of portable and stationary air quality monitors measuring concentrations of greenhouse gases and EPA criteria pollutants using low-cost sensors. The sensors were designed for deployment in Baltimore, MD as part of an EPA SEARCH Center.
